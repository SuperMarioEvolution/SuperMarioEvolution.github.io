My Other NSMBU Hacks

Cloudy Super Mario Bros U

Cloudy SMBU is a NSMBU hack I released back in November 2017, making it my first NSMBU hack, and the first NSMBU hack to go up to and past the final stage.
This is however not a full hack, so don't expect to be able to play throug every stage.

This hack contains:

- 30 Brand new stages
- New and old tilesets
- New level mechanics and themes
- A lot of bugs (it was my first hack after all)

Luigi Saves Christmas

Luigi Saves Christmas is my second hack, which I released durring the holiday season of 2017. Even though it's only a one-world hack, it was the first-ever NSLU hack released.

This hack contains:

- 8+ Brand new stages
- Brand new tilesets
- A christmas-y vibe!
- A few retextures of enemies and objects

Mini Super Mario Bros Wii U

This hack contains:
